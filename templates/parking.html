parking

There will be discounted valet and self-parking available at the Westin Buckhead!

Guests can validate right after arriving at the Parking Services desk by the coffee shop on the bottom floor of the Westin Buckhead

If you decided to valet your car, let them know you're here for the Robbani-Khan wedding!

NOTE: Please do not park at Lenox Mall or at the Shoppes of Lenox and walk to the hotel - they will boot your car!
