FAQ

Am I at the right Westin?

We get it, you tried your best. Make sure that the Westin you are at is titled The Westin Buckhead Atlanta. The address is 3391 Peachtree Rd NE Atlanta, GA 30326 and is near Lenox Square.

What should I wear?

The Mehndi on Friday night is traditionally a colorful event, so dress in your boldest and brightest colors! All are welcome to wear traditional clothing and we are happy to send links if you want help finding outfits :) If you do not want to wear traditional clothing, business casual is perfect. Remember to bring your dancing shoes!

The reception on Saturday night is a traditional, formal affair. Put on your favorite suit or dress - just be sure to leave some space to enjoy a nice dinner. We are so excited to celebrate with you!

Are there any colors I should avoid?

Any and all colors are welcome! Bring your best outfits!

Where should guests park? Is parking free?

You can view the parking information in the parking tab

COVID & vaccinations?

The safety of our family & friends is of the utmost importance. At this time, we will require all guests who are eligible for the COVID-19 vaccine to be fully vaccinated & boosted by our wedding day. We feel this is the best way to ensure the health & safety of all of our guests, including those who may be at risk. We thank you for your understanding. If you have any questions, please feel free to shoot us a text!
